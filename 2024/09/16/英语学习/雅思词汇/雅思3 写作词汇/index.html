写作词汇

一、关联词的使用

表并列

  1. and or
  2. not only but also
  3. meanwhile
  4. at the same time
  5. similarly
  6. likewise

表递进

  1. then
  2. next
  3. besides
  4. furthermore
  5. moreover
  6. what is more
  7. in addition
  8. additionally

表总结

  1. in conclusion
  2. in brief
  3. in general
  4. generally speaking
  5. overall
  6. all in all
  7. to sum up

表强调

  1. in fact
  2. as a matter of fact
  3. indeed
  4. especially
  5. particularly
  6. undoubtedly

举例

  1. for example
  2. for instance
  3. such as(只能加单词)

表因果

因为

  1. due to
  2. owing to
  3. thanks to
  4. because of
  5. as a result of

避免与动词搭配或连词混用，连词加句子，动词搭配还是动词

  1. result from
  2. because

所以

  1. so
    therefore
  2. thus
  3. hence
  4. accordingly
  5. consequently
  6. as a result
  1. cause
  2. lead to
  3. result in
  4. bring about

转折

  1. but yet however
  2. while
  3. on the other hand
  4. on the contrary
  5. nevertheless
  6. whereas

让步（尽管）

  1. although notwithstanding
  2. albeit
  3. it is true that
  4. admittedly
  1. despite

对比

  1. compare

    1. compared with+ 名词
    2. in comparison+ 逗号
    3. comparatively 做副词

  2. by contrast

  3. in contrast
